1. Using Auto Translation

To Auto Translate your forms or strings you need to click in a form listed or in the "Strings" ítem in the "Project list" at the left, then right-click in it. You will see a Popup menu, just click "Auto Translate ". In the Auto Translate window, select the language that you want to translate, select controls or strings and click "Translate". It will take a few moments to finish the translating.
